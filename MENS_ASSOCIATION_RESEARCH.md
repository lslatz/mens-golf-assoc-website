## Resources for Finding Sites

**Google Search Terms to Try:**
- "Boise Ranch men's golf association"
- "Warm Springs golf men's club Boise"
- "[Course Name] men's league Boise"
- "Boise golf men's association"

**Where to Look:**
- Golf course main websites (look for "Men's Club" or "Men's Association" tabs)
- Facebook groups (many associations use social media)
- City of Boise recreation department (for public courses)
- Idaho Golf Association website (may list local associations)

**Contact Information:**
If websites aren't found online, consider calling:
- Boise Ranch Pro Shop
- Warm Springs Pro Shop
- Other course pro shops

They can direct you to men's association websites or provide contact info.

## Important Note

Some men's associations may:
- Not have a website at all (use Facebook only)
- Have a simple page on the course website
- Have a separate dedicated website

Document whatever you find - even "no website found" is useful information!
