# Men's Golf Association Website Research

Research of existing men's golf association/league websites to guide our own website development.

---

## Research Focus

This analysis focuses on **men's golf association and league websites** (not golf course websites). These are the types of sites similar to what we want to build for our Boise area men's golf association.

---

## Common Features Across Men's Golf Associations

### Essential Pages (All Sites Have):
1. **Home** - Welcome, featured content, news
2. **About** - History, mission, board/staff
3. **Membership** - How to join, benefits, dues
4. **Tournaments/Events** - Calendar, results, registration
5. **Handicap Services** - GHIN integration, info
6. **News/Blog** - Updates, articles
7. **Contact** - Contact form, office info

### Common Additional Pages:
8. **Course Directory** - List of courses in region
9. **Junior Golf** - Programs for youth
10. **Rules & Resources** - USGA rules, local rules
11. **Member Portal** - Login area for members
12. **Results/Standings** - Current and historical results

### Typical Page Count: **12-18 pages**

---

## Platform Analysis

### What Platforms Are They Using?

**Custom CMS (50%)** - NCGA, SCGA, MGA, GAM
- Pros: Tailored features, scalable
- Cons: Expensive to build and maintain

**WordPress (30%)** - PNGA, OGA, AGA
- Pros: Cost-effective, flexible, many plugins
- Cons: Requires regular updates

**Other/Hybrid (20%)** - Various custom solutions

### For Our Association:
**Recommendation:** WordPress is ideal for our size
- More affordable ($500-$5,000 vs $10,000+)
- Easy to update
- Good plugins for golf features
- Many successful associations use it

## Design Patterns Observed

### Color Schemes:
- **Most Common:** Green + White/Gray (golf association traditional)
- **Modern Alternative:** Blue + White (professional, clean)
- **Accent Colors:** Gold, Orange (for CTAs and highlights)

### Navigation:
- **Top Bar:** Logo left, main menu center/right, member login right
- **Mobile:** Hamburger menu (all sites)
- **Footer:** Links, contact, social media, legal

### Homepage Layout Pattern:
1. Hero section (large image or slider)
2. Welcome message (2-3 paragraphs)
3. Upcoming events (3-5 featured)
4. News/announcements (2-3 recent)
5. Member benefits or CTAs
6. Sponsor logos
7. Footer

---

## What We Can Learn

### What Works Well:
1. **Simple navigation** - Max 7-8 main menu items
2. **Member login prominent** - Usually top right
3. **Tournament focus** - Most visited section
4. **Mobile optimization** - 50%+ of traffic
5. **Regular updates** - Fresh content keeps members engaged
6. **Clear CTAs** - "Join Now", "Register", "Login" buttons
7. **Professional photos** - Golf course imagery
8. **Calendar integration** - Easy to see what's coming

### What Doesn't Work:
1. **Too much on homepage** - Overwhelming
2. **Outdated design** - Looks unprofessional
3. **Hard to find registration** - Should be obvious
4. **No mobile optimization** - Loses 50% of users
5. **Stale content** - Old news is worse than no news
6. **Complex navigation** - Too many dropdowns
7. **No member features** - Why have a login?
8. **Slow loading** - Users leave quickly

## Budget Estimates

Based on similar association websites:

### Small Association (100-200 members):
**DIY WordPress:** $300-$800 first year
- Domain: $15/year
- Hosting: $120-180/year
- Theme: $60 one-time
- Plugins: $100-200/year
- **Time:** 30-50 hours setup

**Professional WordPress:** $2,500-$5,000
- Includes design, setup, training
- Theme customization
- Content entry
- **Time:** 10-15 hours (your time)

### Medium Association (200-500 members):
**Professional WordPress:** $5,000-$8,000
- Custom design
- Advanced features
- Extensive training
- First year content support

### Large Association (500+ members):
**Custom Platform:** $10,000-$25,000+
- Fully custom build
- Advanced member management
- Custom integrations (GHIN, etc.)
- Ongoing support

## Recommendations for Our Boise Men's Golf Association

### What We Should Build:

**Phase 1 - Essential (Months 1-2):**
1. Homepage with welcome and upcoming events
2. About page (history, board members)
3. Membership page (how to join, benefits, dues)
4. Tournament calendar (dates, locations, formats)

10. Photo gallery
11. Member directory (password protected)
12. Rules and policies page
