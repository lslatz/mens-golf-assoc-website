## Essential Website Pages (8-12 pages)

1. **Home** - Welcome, upcoming events, news
2. **About** - History, mission, board members
3. **Membership** - Benefits, dues, how to join
4. **Events** - Calendar, tournament schedule
5. **Results** - Standings, past results
6. **News** - Announcements, updates
7. **Contact** - Contact form, officer info
8. **Rules** - Local rules, policies

**Optional but Recommended:**
- Sponsors page
- Photo gallery
- Member directory (password-protected)
- Resources (handicap info, USGA rules)
