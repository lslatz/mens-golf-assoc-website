## Free Stock Photo Sources

### Recommended Websites:

1. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com/)
   - Search: "golf course", "golf", "putting green"
   - All photos are free for commercial use
   - High quality professional photography

2. **Pexels** (https://pexels.com/)
   - Search: "golf", "golf course", "golf tournament"
   - Free for commercial use
   - Good variety of golf-related images

3. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com/)
   - Search: "golf", "golf course"
   - Free for commercial use
   - Wide selection of images

### Image Guidelines:

- **Quality:** Use high-resolution images (minimum specified dimensions)
- **Style:** Modern, bright, professional golf course photography
- **Color:** Natural colors, well-lit scenes, blue skies preferred
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered images that work well as backgrounds
- **Format:** JPEG format with good compression (target 200-500KB per image)

### Optimization:

Before adding images to the repository:
1. Resize to exact dimensions specified
2. Optimize file size using tools like:
   - TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com/)
   - ImageOptim (for macOS)
   - GIMP or Photoshop "Save for Web"
3. Target file sizes: 200-500KB for best performance
