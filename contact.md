### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What skill level do I need to join?**  
A: All skill levels are welcome! We have golfers ranging from beginners to advanced players.

**Q: How do I register for events?**  
A: All event registration is handled through our <a href="{{ site.golf_genius_url }}" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Golf Genius portal</a>.

**Q: When are events held?**  
A: We typically have weekly events during the golf season (April-October). Check our <a href="{{ site.golf_genius_url }}" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Golf Genius schedule</a> for specific dates and times.

**Q: Do I need a handicap?**  
A: While not required to join, having a handicap helps ensure fair competition. Our Golf Genius system can help you establish and maintain your handicap.
