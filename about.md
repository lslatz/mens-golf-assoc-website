## About Indian Lakes Golf Course Men's Association

The Indian Lakes Golf Course Men's Association is a vibrant community of golf enthusiasts located in Boise, Idaho. Our association provides members with regular competitive and recreational golf opportunities throughout the season.

### Our Mission

To foster a welcoming environment for golfers of all skill levels, promote friendly competition, and strengthen the bonds within our golfing community.

### Membership Benefits

- Access to weekly tournaments and events
- Handicap tracking and management through Golf Genius
- Competitive league play
- Social events and gatherings
- Member pricing on select events
- Connection with fellow golf enthusiasts

### Season Information

Our season typically runs from April through October, with weekly events and special tournaments scheduled throughout the year.

### Golf Genius Integration

We use Golf Genius to manage all aspects of our association including:
- Tournament registration and management
- Real-time scoring and leaderboards
- Handicap tracking and calculation
- Event schedules and tee times
- Player statistics and history

All members can access our Golf Genius portal to:
- View upcoming events
- Register for tournaments
- Check current standings
- Review past results
- Update their player profile
